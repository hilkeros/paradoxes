greta

The last year we have been spending a lot of time in front of our webcams. Hilke's single debut 'Greta', featuring vocals by Gregory Frateur (Dez Mona), is not only a song, but an interactive web experience, which makes you think about how we express feelings on the internet.

Call your friends, switch your cameras on, show off your lip syncing skills, or just dance on hilk.eu/greta (work in progress for now...)
